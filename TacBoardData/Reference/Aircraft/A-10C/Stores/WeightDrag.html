Notes

Store weights and drag indices are used in performance calculations to determine the expected aircraft performance based on the mission loadout.

NOTE: The DCS loadout UI automatically calculates and displays the total aircraft weight. Drag index must be calculated manually.

Weight

  • Basic aircraft weights are as follows:
    • Empty Aircraft: 24967 lb
    • Empty Aircraft + Full Fuel: 36054 lb
    • Empty Aircraft + Full Fuel + Full Cannon Ammo: 37829 lb
  • To determine the total aircraft weight, start with the appropriate basic weight from the above table, and then add the sum of the weights of all equipped stores (including equipment racks where applicable).

Drag Index

  • Drag index of empty aircraft is equal to 0.0.
  • To determine the total drag index, sum the drag indices for all equipped stores (including equipment racks where applicable).
