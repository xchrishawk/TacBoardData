General Purpose

Mk-82
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Triple Ejector Rack ×3
Usage Unarmored / Lightly Armored Targets
Notes
  • Low-drag general-purpose (LDGP) bomb.
  • Weight 500 lbs with 129 lbs of Tritonal HE.
  • Serves as basis for multiple other bomb types, including Mk-82AIR, GBU-12, GBU-38, and all BDU-50 variants.
Mk-82AIR
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Triple Ejector Rack ×3
Usage Unarmored / Lightly Armored Targets (Low Altitude)
Notes
  • Mk-82 fitted with high-drag tail "ballute" to allow additional escape time for aircraft.
  • May be launched in low-drag or high-drag modes, depending on fuze settings (N for low drag, N/T and T for high drag).
Mk-84
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Unarmored / Lightly Armored Targets
Notes
  • Larger version of Mk-82 LDGP.
  • Weight 2039 lbs with 945 lbs of Tritonal HE.
  • Serves as basis for multiple other bomb types, including GBU-10 and GBU-31.

Cluster

CBU-87
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Unarmored / Armored Targets
Notes
  • All-purpose cluster bomb containing 202 BLU-97/B Combined Effects Bomblets (CEBs).
  • Dispersal footprint depends on Height of Function (HOF) and spin RPM settings in DSMS. Standard footprint is approximately 200 × 400 meters.
  • Effective against tank top armor due to the high attack angle.
CBU-97
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Armor
Notes
  • Anti-armor cluster bomb containing 10 BLU-108/B bomblets, each of which contains 4 "puck-shaped" skeet infrared sensing projectiles.
  • Designed specifically to attack armor concentrations.
CBU-103
Guidance Inertial
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Unarmored / Armored Targets
Notes
  • Inertially-guided (WCMD) version of CBU-87 all-purpose cluster bomb.
CBU-105
Guidance Inertial
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Armor
Notes
  • Inertially-guided (WCMD) version of CBU-97 anti-armor cluster bomb.

Laser Guided

GBU-10
Guidance Laser
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Usage Precision Anti-Infrastructure
Notes
  • Paveway II laser-guided bomb based on 2000-lb Mk-84 unguided bomb.
  • Laser guidance provides extreme accuracy, including against moving targets.
  • Suitable for use against large, hardened targets such as bridges, fortifications, and command posts.
GBU-12
Guidance Laser
Compatibility Single Ejector Rack ×1
Triple Ejector Rack ×3
Usage Precision Anti-Armor
Notes
  • Paveway II laser-guided bomb based on 500-lb Mk-82 unguided bomb.
  • Laser guidance provides extreme accuracy, including against moving targets.
  • Suitable for use against armor.

JDAM

GBU-31
Guidance Inertial/GPS
Compatibility 1760 Station ×1
Usage Precision Anti-Infrastructure
Notes
  • Mk-84 2000-lb bomb fitted with JDAM guidance kit.
  • Capable of accurately striking targets at night and through inclement weather.
  • Target coordinates cannot be changed after launch - not suitable against moving targets.
  • Suitable for use against large, hardened targets such as bridges, fortifications, and command posts.
GBU-31(V)3/B
Guidance Inertial/GPS
Compatibility 1760 Station ×1
Usage Bunker Buster
Notes
  • BLU-109 "bunker buster" 2000-lb bomb equipped with JDAM guidance kit.
  • Used against hardened targets such as bunkers.
GBU-38
Guidance Inertial/GPS
Compatibility 1760 Station ×1
Usage Precision Anti-Armor
Notes
  • Mk-82 500-lb bomb fitted with JDAM guidance kit.
  • Capable of accurately striking targets at night and through inclement weather.
  • Target coordinates cannot be changed after launch - not suitable against moving targets.
  • Suitable for use against armor.

Flares

LUU-2
Guidance Unguided
Compatibility SUU-25 ×8
Usage Illumination
Notes
  • Flare providing battlefield illumination for non-NVIG equipped forces.
  • Flare provides illumination over a 500 meter circular area at an altitude of 1000 feet. Flare burns for approximately five minutes.
  • Equipped with a parachute to slow rate of fall.
