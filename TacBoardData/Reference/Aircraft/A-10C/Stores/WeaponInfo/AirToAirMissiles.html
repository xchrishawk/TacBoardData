Infrared

AIM-9L
Guidance Infrared
Compatibility LAU-105 ×2
Usage Short Range Air-to-Air
Notes
  • AIM-9L Sidewinder short range, heat-seeking, air-to-air missile for defense against air targets.
  • Uses infrared seeker in nose of the missile to track targets. Susceptible to countermeasure flares.
AIM-9M
Guidance Infrared
Compatibility LAU-105 ×2
Usage Short Range Air-to-Air
Notes
  • AIM-9M Sidewinder short range, heat-seeking, air-to-air missile for defense against air targets.
  • Uses infrared seeker in nose of the missile to track targets. Susceptible to countermeasure flares.
  • AIM-9M is an updated version of the AIM-9L with an improved rocket motor and electronics.

Training

CAP-9M
Guidance Infrared
Compatibility LAU-105 ×2
Usage Training
Notes
  • Training version of AIM-9M missile with inert warhead and rocket motor.
