Notes

RWR symbols can be in one of three states:

  • No Circle: Radar is in acquisition/search mode.
  • Steady Circle: Radar is tracking/locked on to your aircraft.
  • Flashing Circle: Radar is supporting a missile which has been launched at your aircraft.

Audio tones will be played for the following events:

  • New Threat: Played when a new radar emitter is detected.
  • Radar Lock: Played when a radar has locked on to your aircraft.
  • Missile Launch: Played when a missile has launched. Does not necessarily indicate that the missile is targeting your aircraft.
